SERVICES

OUR SERVICES

01

Records and Archives Management

PRAAD provides professional records and archives management services for government ministries, departments, agencies, and public institutions across Ghana. We ensure that official records are properly created, organized, stored, maintained, and disposed of in accordance with national policies and legal requirements.

02

Preservation and Conservation

PRAAD is responsible for preserving Ghana's valuable historical documents and archival materials for future generations. Through conservation techniques, secure storage systems, and environmental protection measures, we safeguard important records from deterioration, damage, and loss.

03

Public Access & Reference Services

PRAAD offers access to archival records for researchers, students, public institutions, historians, journalists, and the general public. Our reference services support academic research, policy development, legal verification, genealogy, and historical studies.

04

Digital Records Management

To support modern governance and digital transformation, PRAAD promotes the management and preservation of electronic records. We assist public institutions with digital records systems, electronic document preservation, digitization initiatives, and best practices for secure digital information management.

05

Advisory and Consultancy Services

PRAAD provides expert guidance and consultancy services on records management policies, filing systems, retention schedules, archives administration, and compliance with records management standards. We help organizations establish effective systems that support operational efficiency.

06

Training and Capacity Building

PRAAD organizes training programs, workshops, and professional development initiatives to strengthen the capacity of records officers and public sector staff. Our training focuses on modern records management practices, archives administration, digital preservation, and information governance.

07

Records Appraisal and Disposal

PRAAD evaluates public records to determine their long-term historical, administrative, legal, or financial value. We supervise the proper retention and lawful disposal of records to ensure that important national documents are preserved while unnecessary records are securely removed.

08

Archival Research Support

Researchers and institutions can rely on PRAAD for assistance in locating and accessing historical records and government archives. Our archival professionals provide research support services that help users identify relevant materials efficiently and accurately.

THE ONBOARDING

01 Data Collection and Records Preparation

Existing physical and electronic records are gathered, reviewed, and prepared for migration into the PRAAD system. This process includes records sorting, classification, indexing, file verification, metadata assignment, and identification of records for digitization or archival preservation.

02 Digitization and Data Migration

Physical records are scanned and converted into digital formats where necessary. Existing electronic records and databases are securely migrated into the new system while maintaining data integrity, accuracy, and accessibility throughout the transition process.

03 System Integration and Testing

The configured system undergoes testing to ensure all modules, workflows, security controls, and retrieval functions operate effectively. Integration with existing institutional systems may also be carried out to support seamless information management and operational continuity.

04 User Training and Capacity Building

PRAAD provides comprehensive training sessions for records officers, administrators, and institutional staff. Training covers system usage, records management procedures, digital workflows, security protocols, document retrieval, and compliance responsibilities to ensure smooth adoption.

05 Pilot Deployment and Validation

A pilot implementation is conducted within selected departments or units to evaluate system performance under real operational conditions. Feedback is collected, adjustments are made where necessary, and final validations are completed before full deployment.

06 Full System Deployment

After successful testing and validation, the PRAAD solution is fully deployed across the institution. All approved users gain access to the system, and records management operations officially transition into the new environment.

07 Monitoring and Post-Implementation Support

PRAAD provides continuous technical support, monitoring, and maintenance services after deployment. This includes system updates, troubleshooting, performance monitoring, user assistance, backup management, and ongoing advisory services to ensure long-term operational success.

08 Compliance Review and Continuous Improvement

Periodic evaluations are conducted to ensure the institution remains compliant with records management policies and archival standards. PRAAD also supports continuous improvement initiatives through system enhancements, policy updates, and advanced records management recommendations.
