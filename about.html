About

The PRAAD Management System is transforming how Ghana preserves and accesses its public records. Built as a unified digital platform, it securely stores records from across government institutions, makes them instantly searchable, and ensures that the right people can access the right information at the right time.

This is more than digitization. It is a national shift toward efficient governance, institutional transparency, and modern public service delivery in Ghana.

The PRAAD Management System is designed to meet the realities of Ghana’s public sector. Balancing accessibility, security, scalability, and long-term preservation of national records.

Who can access PRAAD?

  • Government Institutions
  • Private Institutions
  • Individuals

Our Mission

To preserve Ghana’s documentary heritage and provide efficient records and archival management services through professionalism, innovation, and modern technology.

Our Vision

To become a centre of excellence in records and archival management, preserving the nation’s history for present and future generations.

With PRAAD:

  • Public records are digitized and centrally managed
  • Searches that once took days can happen in minutes
  • Ministries and agencies can securely submit and retrieve records online
  • Citizens experience faster, more reliable access to information
  • Record preservation and accountability are significantly improved

Preserving Ghana's history, one document at a time. The official digital gateway to the Public Records and Archives Administration Department.
