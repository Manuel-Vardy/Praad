FEATURES & MODULES

Centralized Records Management

The Centralized Records Management Module enables government institutions to efficiently create, capture, classify, organize, and manage official records throughout their lifecycle.

Digital Archives & Repository

This module provides secure digital storage for archival records and historical documents. It allows authorized users to preserve, access, and retrieve digitized records electronically.

Records Classification System

PRAAD uses a structured classification and indexing system to organize records according to departments, functions, dates, and categories for improved search accuracy.

Records Retention & Disposal

The retention and disposal module helps institutions manage records according to approved retention schedules and legal requirements while ensuring historically valuable documents are preserved.

Archival Preservation

This module focuses on the long-term preservation of physical and electronic records. It includes conservation procedures, environmental monitoring, and restoration support.

Public Access Portal

The public access portal allows researchers, students, and citizens to request and access archival information through controlled public access to non-confidential records.

Electronic Records (ERMS)

The ERMS module supports the management of electronic records and digital workflows within public institutions, enabling secure document sharing and version control.

Document Tracking

This feature enables institutions to monitor the movement and status of records and files in real time, reducing delays and improving operational efficiency.

Security & Access Control

Advanced security measures to protect records from unauthorized access. Includes user authentication, access permissions, and data protection controls.

Digitization & Scanning

Supports the conversion of physical records into electronic formats through high-quality scanning, enhancing preservation efforts and improving accessibility.

Reporting & Analytics

Generates reports on records management activities, archival usage, and institutional compliance to support informed decision-making across departments.

Training & Capacity

Educational support for records officers and public sector staff, including workshops and guidelines to strengthen records management capabilities.

Disaster Recovery & Backup

Protect archival information against cyber threats and system failures with secure data recovery and long-term preservation solutions.

Policy Management

Helping public institutions comply with records management laws, policies, and regulatory frameworks through effective governance standards.

Step 1 — Create your account

Getting started takes only a few minutes. Sign up with your email address or log in using your Ghana Card where available. Government staff can also access the platform using their existing ministry credentials, making onboarding seamless across public institutions.

Step 2 — Search, upload, or connect

Citizens can easily search for and request public records from a single platform. MDA officers can upload, transfer, and manage records digitally, while partner institutions integrate directly through secure APIs. Every user only sees the records and actions they are authorized to access. Ensuring privacy, security, and compliance at every level.

Step 3 — Access what you need

Open records can be downloaded instantly, while restricted requests are reviewed and communicated directly by email. Users can track the status of every request, submission, and approval from a personalized dashboard, bringing transparency and convenience to the entire records process.

Preserving Ghana's history, one document at a time. The official digital gateway to the Public Records and Archives Administration Department.
